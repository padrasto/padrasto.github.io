I'm Padrasto — a self-taught Linux practitioner based in  Portugal, with around 10 years of hands-on experience running and breaking Linux systems.

This blog is where I document real problems and real fixes: things I've actually done on my homelab, my VMs, and production-grade setups. No padding, no fluff.

## What I work with

- **Distros:** Debian, Ubuntu Server, Arch, Proxmox
- **Virtualisation:** KVM/QEMU, Docker, LXC
- **Web & networking:** nginx, SSL/TLS, reverse proxies, WireGuard VPN
- **Databases:** MySQL — setup, backup, recovery, access control
- **Monitoring & automation:** systemd, journalctl, Bash scripting, cron
- **Self-hosting:** Jellyfin, Immich, Nextcloud, Homarr, and more
- **Security:** UFW, Fail2ban, SSH hardening, Tor, LUKS

## What I do

I offer Linux support and setup services for individuals and small businesses — remotely, via Upwork or direct contact.

Typical work:
- VPS setup and hardening
- nginx configuration and troubleshooting
- MySQL installation, backup, and recovery
- WireGuard VPN deployment
- Docker stack deployment and maintenance
- General Linux sysadmin support (Tier 1/2)
